Freemit Privacy Policy

Last Updates: Monday 16 May 2016

Information We Collect

Verification Information - Information that you provide during a completed, incomplete or abandoned registration. This may include:

  • Individual - Your full legal name, physical home address, date of birth, country of birth and/or current nationality, and a valid email address.
  • Device Information - Information that is automatically collected about your device (such as hardware, operating system, browser, etc.).
  • Location Information - Information that is automatically collected via analytics systems providers to determine your location, including your IP address and/or domain name and any external page that referred you to us.
  • Log Information - Information that is generated by your use of the Freemit App that is automatically collected and stored in our server logs. This may include device-specific information, location information, system activity and any internal and external Freemit App pages that you visit.
  • Account Information - Information that is generated by your account activity including Transaction activity, Withdrawals, Deposits, and Exchange history.

In addition, we make use of the accepted practice of placing Cookies (a tiny data file) on your device to help us recognize you.

How we use information we collect

The information collected and the practices described above are done in an effort to provide you with the best experience possible, protect you from risks related to improper use and fraud, and help us maintain and improve Freemit App.

We may share information with trusted and contracted service providers that help us operate our platform, systems and detect fraud and security threats. In addition, we may be compelled to share information with law enforcement, government officials and regulators.

In the event of a merger, acquisition or asset sale of Freemit App, we will give you notice before your information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We do not provide information to third-parties for the purposes of marketing.

Information security

We take your privacy very seriously and work hard to protect Freemit App and you from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we collect and store. In particular, we encrypt our online platform with SSL; we review information collection, storage and processing practices; and we restrict access to your information on a need to know basis to Freeport Mobile employees, contractors and agents who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.
